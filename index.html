OUR LOVE STORY

We first met as elementary school classmates — Niño, the quiet and shy one, and Faye, the talkative and outgoing one. Our neighbors used to tease us together, not knowing it was God's gentle way of leading us to each other.

Years later, in 2021, a simple conversation turned into a beautiful love story — shared adventures, late-night talks, and dreams of a future together. After being in relationship for four years, we soon realized we had found something special.

Through every challenge and joy, our bond grew stronger, confirming that we were not just partners but best friends. We've learned that love isn't just about the big moments, but also the small, everyday ones that make life sweeter.

Today, we joyfully begin this new chapter of our lives, surrounded by those who mean the most to us.
